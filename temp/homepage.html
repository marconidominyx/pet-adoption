About Us

Oasis Pets

Oasis Pet is a community-driven organization born out of the belief that every animal deserves a safe haven — a place to heal, to be loved, and to belong.

Founded in 2025, Oasis Pet began as a small initiative in Butuan City, sparked by the growing number of abandoned and neglected pets in the area. What started as a simple act of kindness turned into a movement of compassion.

Today, Oasis Pet continues to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome cats and dogs who have been forgotten, mistreated, or left behind. Every pawprint matters. Every life counts.
